Undergraduate Research Opportunities
If you have an interest in biology and computers, consider joining the Buell lab to do undergraduate research! Our lab performs bioinformatic analyses on large-scale genetic and genomic datasets. Students in the Buell lab are engaged in research projects in which they learn programming, bioinformatics, large-scale data management, genetics, genomics and biology. If you are majoring in a biological science and/or a computation-oriented discipline, please contact C. Robin Buell with your resume and statement of interest.
