Andrew Wiersma

As a plant breeder, I am passionate about developing high-quality and productive crop varieties with enhanced nutrition, flavor, disease resistance, and abiotic stress tolerance. New crop varieties have the potential to transform sustainable agricultural systems, strengthen industries, and improve human nutrition and health. My interests in genomics-enabled breeding tools and technologies led me to the Buell Lab, where I am continuing my research on breeding crops with durable disease resistance. During my spare time, I enjoy traveling with my wife, backpacking, gardening, and volunteering with my local historic society.
