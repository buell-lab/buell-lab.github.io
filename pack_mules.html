Pack-MULEs and Genome Evolution
in Rice

Pack-MULEs are transposable elements that carry fragments of normal protein-coding genes. NSF funding supports a project on the impact of Pack-MULEs on plant genome evolution and mechanisms of sequence acquisition by Pack-MULEs.

  • Ning Jiang, PhD, Michigan State University
  • C. Robin Buell, PhD, Michigan State University
  • Yuehua Cui, PhD, Michigan State University
